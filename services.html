Require DTCP Approval?

At NorthPoint, we manage the complete DTCP (Directorate of Town and Country Planning) approval process for your property or development project. From preparing and submitting all necessary documentation to securing final approval, our expert team ensures every legal and planning standard is met efficiently and accurately, helping your project move forward smoothly without delays.

Legal and Documentation

At NorthPoint, we ensure a smooth and secure property transaction. We handle due diligence, verifying ownership, checking disputes, and clearing any existing loans. Our land survey confirms property dimensions and surroundings match official documents, and our legal verification ensures all paperwork is clear, so you can proceed with registration confidently.

Buying & Selling

At NorthPoint, we provide complete support in buying and selling properties, including thorough legal analysis and verification. We identify lands with overdue loans, negotiate with banks and owners, and create amicable proposals. With all necessary checks and paperwork, we ensure the property is securely registered under our client’s name.

Loans and subsidy

At NorthPoint, we provide loan assistance to help you purchase the property of your choice. Beyond verifying and guiding you on the property, our experts assist with all formalities for property loans. With a Direct Selling Agent from a leading bank as our partner, we make securing financial support easier and hassle-free.

Property Management

At NorthPoint, we offer complete property management services to give you peace of mind, even when you’re away. From finding tenants and closing rental agreements to property maintenance, tax payments, and more, our customized approach ensures your property is well cared for with minimal effort on your part.

Project Management

At NorthPoint, we provide end-to-end land and project management for residential and industrial developments. From studying the land and its surroundings to ensure it fits your project plan, to developing basic infrastructure for DTCP compliance, we handle it all. We also assist with detailed development proposals and provide loan and financial support to make your project a reality.
