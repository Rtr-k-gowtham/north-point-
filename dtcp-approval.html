What is DTCP Approval?

DTCP (Directorate of Town and Country Planning) approval is a mandatory clearance required for any land development or layout project in Tamil Nadu. This approval ensures that your property development complies with local planning regulations, building codes, and zoning laws.

At Northpoint, we specialize in navigating the complex DTCP approval process, ensuring your project meets all regulatory requirements while minimizing delays and complications.
